Return Policy

Standard Return Policy

Return requests may be submitted within 30 days for eligible unworn merchandise. Made-to-order sizes, styles marked as final sale, complimentary gifts with purchase, gift cards, merchandise credits and shipping fees are not eligible. The return label provided is covered under our carrier policy. If you choose to use your own return label, John Hardy is not responsible for additional duties and taxes, orders lost in transit, or packages held by U.S. customs.

Biannual sale products are marked as final sale and are not eligible for return or exchange.

All returns will be refunded to the original method of payment on your order. For applicable returns a merchandise credit will be issued.

If you received your John Hardy item as a gift, please email our Client Care team at CLIENTCARE@JOHNHARDY.COM or call 888.838.3022 for assistance.

U.S. Return Instructions:

To request a return, please click HERE.

1. Domestic orders can print a complimentary FedEx return label.

2. Include your return packing slip along with your unworn merchandise and place it securely in the original packaging.

3. Affix the pre-paid return label to the outside of the box and bring it to your local FedEx shipping center. Please note the return tracking number for your reference. .

International Return Instructions:

To request a return, please click HERE.

2. Affix your return shipping label to the package and note the return tracking for your reference. DHL will electronically share the clearance documents with Customs.

3. Please drop your package off at the nearest DHL shipping center.

Please note, all duties and taxes paid are nonrefundable.
